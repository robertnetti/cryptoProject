Affine

The Affine cipher is type of the general monoalphabetic substitution cipher. It is less secure than a substitution cipher. Its main weakness is that through means such as frequency analysis, brute force, guessing or otherwise, the plaintext of two ciphertext characters can be found, then the key can be obtained by solving a simultaneous equation
